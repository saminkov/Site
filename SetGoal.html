Want to start following a new goal?
Keep in mind that your goal should be SMART:
S: Specific - Define precisely what you want to do
M: Measurable - You should be able to measure how far you are from achieving your target
A: Achievable - Dream big, but dream realistically
R: Relevant - Make sure the goal is result-oriented
T: Time-bound - Set a deadline
